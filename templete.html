About Evergreen

A Legacy of Excellence,
A Future of Innovation

Founded in 1892, Evergreen University stands as a beacon of academic achievement, research innovation, and social responsibility. Our 285-acre campus is home to 42,000 students from 180+ countries, united by a shared commitment to discovery and impact.
